So what really is a Cowegg?

Have you ever played a video game? And found an easter egg that the game developers left in the game? The same applies here at Coweggs! The Cowegg itself is the html page, and to unlock it, one must find the secret unlocking sequence to claim ownership of the Cowegg. The secret sequence can be an order of clicks of a certain element. It could be an input into a dialog box. To find the secret sequence, study the page, look at all the elements of the page, and use clues to determine what the secret may be. ANYONE, yes literally anyone, can find the secret to the Cowegg! There is no knowlege of code, or computer science knowlege needed to find the secret. Coweggs is for everyone!

How long do I have to find the secret?

One minute of time costs 1 USD$. You can pay and play as much as you want. Once your time runs out, you will be prompted to pay for more time, or quit and try again later.
